Design highlight of the month

This bead necklace is a design handmade in Uppsala, Sweden by Designer X. A jewellery designer with more than 40 years of experience in the business

Our philosophy about handmade jewelry is literally just that, made by the “hands” of the artisan or maker. The pieces are soldered, sawed, carved, and shaped without the use of manufacturing machinery. A machine can crank out hundreds of units per hour while an individual can only make a finite quantity. Why does this matter? Attention to detail, my friends! Your handmade jewelry will be far less likely to have flaws and imperfections than something made in bulk.
